### How to play

Explore the colony on LV4-2600

Avoid Xenomorphs (or shoot the shit out of them)

Rescue Newt

Defeat the Xenomorph Queen

Get back to the dropship before the colony asplode

Punch-Out in the landing bay: Loader Vs Queen!
